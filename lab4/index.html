Task 1 - pseudocode for an everyday task: creating a colored drawing

  1. Sketch your subject
    1. Roughly position simple basic shapes to represent volumes
    2. Mark location of significant details
  2. Flat colors
    1. Determine color scheme or palette
    2. Fill based on sketched volumes
  3. Establish basic values
    1. Determine source of light
    2. Determine placement of light and shadow based on source of light and sketched volumes
  4. Refine shapes and detail
    1. Add detail to smaller portions of the piece
    2. Step back and evaluate
    3. Iterate this step until satisfied with piece
  5. Sign when done :D
